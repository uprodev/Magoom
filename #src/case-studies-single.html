Transforming Frontu: A Strategic Rebrand and Site Migration

In the ever-evolving digital landscape, standing out becomes not just an advantage but a necessity. Frontu, a frontiersperson in Field Service Management (FSM) software, faced a key moment: the need for a digital transition. Our mission? To not only elevate Frontu's brand but to redefine its digital presence, steering it towards growth and clarity in its market position. Here's how we turned challenges into achievements.

  • Challenge

    Frontu (formerly TaskerTools) stood at a crossroads, restrained by digital barriers that suppressed its growth:

    • SEO improvements: Climbing the SEO ladder with the current stack seemed overpriced, and improvements were nearly impossible to achieve.

    • Domain and trademark limitations: Without owning the .com domain and trademark, Frontu's digital footprint was constrained.

    • Brand confusion: Commonly confused with another B2C brand and the task management tool, Frontu's identity was diluted, impacting the quality of Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs).

  • Strategic Overhaul

    Our proposed strategy was not just a facelift but a heart transplant for Frontu's brand. We ventured on a journey to:

    • Refine the Brand Essence: We diligently crafted a brand that truly resonates with Field Service Management, sharpening a tone of voice that focused directly on frontline workers.

    • Enhanced Inbound Marketing: We rebuilt Frontu's site structure through extensive research, making it a beacon for relevant visitors and a powerhouse for SEO.

    • Championed Transparent Communication: Change can be rough. We ensured every stakeholder, both internally and externally, understood the 'why' behind every decision, turning potential resistance into unanimous support.
