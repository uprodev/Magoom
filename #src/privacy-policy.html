Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy was last updated on 18th February 2024.

This document outlines the methods and principles governing the collection, processing, and sharing of your personal data by us. It is important for you to review this document to grasp our approach and handling of your personal data.

What kind of data do we collect from you?

We gather and process data about you in the following categories:

  • Data you provide: This includes personal details shared through our website's request forms, communications via phone, email, or other means, and information provided when you search for a service, place an order, contact us through our website, or report a website issue. This data may encompass your name, address, email address, and phone number
  • Data we automatically collect: Each visit to our website may result in the collection of technical information (such as your IP address, browser details, and operating system), as well as details of your visit (like the pages you interact with and how you navigate away from our site). We may use cookies to facilitate this collection.
  • Data from your use of our services: This includes information about you, your employees, or your contacts, which might consist of names, job titles, and contact details.
  • Data from other sources: We might receive data about you from third parties, including our business partners and service providers.

How do we use your data?

We utilise the collected information to fulfil our contractual obligations to you, to offer and improve our services, to provide customer support, and for marketing purposes. We also use it to manage and secure our website and applications, and to personalise our offerings to you.

Disclosure of your information

We may share your personal data within our corporate group and with selected third parties such as business partners and service providers to fulfil any contracts with you, for analytics and search optimisation, and where required by law or to protect our rights.

Cookies

Our website employs cookies to enhance your browsing experience, differentiate you from other users, and optimise our site's functionality. You have the option to block cookies, but this may limit your access to certain parts of our site.

Data storage and security

Your data may be stored and processed outside the European Economic Area (EEA), and we are committed to ensuring its secure and lawful handling.

Your rights

You have rights concerning your personal data, including the right to object to marketing uses, to amend or withdraw your consent for data processing, and to access the information we hold about you.

External links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites, which are governed by their own privacy policies.

Privacy policy changes

We may update this privacy policy, with changes posted on our website. We encourage you to review it regularly. For further information or to exercise your data protection rights, please contact us at hi@magoom.com.
